# Storyboard — Vivid Lingua 30s Vertical Ad (Mongolian)

Format: 1080×1920 (9:16), ~30s, no audio track in v1.
Visual language: Aurora Atelier (violet-ink night sky #060512, aurora violet #b388fa,
ice blue #66aafd, polar rose #fb6d8e, text #f2f0fa). Fonts: Unbounded (display),
Golos Text (body) — Google Fonts, full Cyrillic.

## Scene 1 — Hook (0.0–4.0s)
Dark night-sky background with subtle aurora gradient drift.
Kinetic typography, line-by-line slide-up:
- «Герман хэл сурмаар байна уу?» (Unbounded, large)
- «Гэхдээ хаанаас эхлэхээ мэдэхгүй?» (smaller, on-surface-variant #a8a3c8)
Word emphasis: «Герман» in violet #b388fa.

## Scene 2 — Product reveal (4.0–9.0s)
Phone frame (HTML mockup, rounded-corner device shell, soft glow shadow) springs up
from bottom with slight overshoot. Inside: app home screen mockup — dark UI,
violet accents, German word card visible.
Headline above phone: «Vivid Lingua» (Unbounded)
Sub: «Герман хэлийг монголоор сур» — slide-up after phone lands.

## Scene 3 — Feature montage (9.0–22.0s) — 3 sub-scenes, ~4.3s each
Each: phone mockup swaps content (slide-left transition), feature label chip animates in.

3a. **Түвшин тогтоох тест** (placement test)
   UI: question card + 4 answer buttons, progress bar fills, level badge «B1» pops.
3b. **Ухаалаг флашкарт** (smart flashcards)
   UI: card flips — front «die Reise», back «аялал» — second card flies in;
   spaced-repetition arrows hint repeat cycle.
3c. **Шууд орчуулга** (instant tooltips)
   UI: German sentence, finger-tap ripple on word «Wörterbuch», tooltip bubble pops
   with Mongolian gloss «толь бичиг».

## Scene 4 — Progression (22.0–26.0s)
Level ladder animates: A1 → A2 → B1 → B2 → C1 → C2 chips light up sequentially
left-to-right in gradient violet→blue→rose. Label: «A1-ээс C2 хүртэл».

## Scene 5 — CTA (26.0–30.0s)
Logo lockup «Vivid Lingua» scales up center. Tagline: «Өнөөдөр эхэл».
Button mockup «Эхлэх →» with pulse animation. URL line under (placeholder:
vividlingua.app — confirm real URL).
Aurora background brightens slightly for the close.

## Motion rules
- Entrances: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) (app's fade-in curve) or spring overshoot
  cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) (app's scale-up curve)
- Nothing static longer than 3s; background aurora always drifting
- Text per-line stagger 120–180ms
